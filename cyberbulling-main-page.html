Cyberbullying is a problem we can solve

Cyberbullying is serious and affects young people every day. Three out of ten Canadian youth say they have been cyberbullied.. Cyberbullying hurts people and can have a negative impact on the self-esteem, relationships, and future of those involved.

There are things you can do to prevent and deal with cyberbullying if it happens to you or to someone you care about. Together, we can stop cyberbullying.
